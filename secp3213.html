Coursework, projects, and reflections exploring data warehousing, ETL workflows,
and visual analytics dashboards for SECP3213.

This course introduces students to the concepts and tools essential for transforming raw data into actionable business insights. Students will explore business intelligence fundamentals, data warehousing, ETL processes, dashboard design, and predictive analytics. The course also provides hands-on experience with industry-standard BI tools, including Alteryx, Tableau, and Power BI.
